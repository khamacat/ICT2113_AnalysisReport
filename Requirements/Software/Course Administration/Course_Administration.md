**BC - L0 Course Administration**
  - **BCM - L1.1 Course & Subject Management (Essential)**  
    Administrative staff shall oversee the setup and organisation of all courses and their related subjects.
  - **BRE - L1.2 Student Registration & Enrollment (Essential)**  
    Administrative staff shall register students and assign them to the appropriate courses and subjects.
  - **BRS - L1.3 Register staff (Essential)**  
    Administrative staff shall assign registered teaching staff to the appropriate courses and subjects.
