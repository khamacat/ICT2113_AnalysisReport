# Customer-Provided Models

This folder contains modelling artefacts that were supplied directly by the customer.  
They represent the baseline understanding of system requirements and processes.

## Contents
- **DFD.png** – Data Flow Diagram provided by the customer.
- **gantt_chart.png** – Project timeline (Gantt chart) proposed by the customer.
- **use_case.png** – Use Case diagram provided by the customer.
- **Paper_Napkin1.png** – Paper prototype sketch for the proposed web application.
- **Paper_Napkin2.png** – Simple workflow and clarification notes with the Account Manager.
- **Data_Dictionary.txt** – Tabular definition of entities, attributes, data types, and relationships supplied by the customer.

## Purpose
- Serve as the **baseline models** for analysis.
- Provide initial context for requirements and project planning.
- Highlight areas where further clarification or refinement may be needed.

## Notes
- These artefacts are not final and may contain ambiguities.
- The team will review and refine them in the `team_developed` folder.
