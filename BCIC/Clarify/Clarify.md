## Tracking Clarifications
Detailed records of each incongruity, its resolution status, and stakeholder input have been logged in the **Issues tab** of this repository.  
- **Resolved incongruities** are marked as *Closed*.  
- **Open incongruities** remain *Open* until further stakeholder input is received.  
- Each Issue includes the requirement ID, the incongruity detected, and a summary of the resolution or pending clarification.

Refer to the Issues tab for the full list of clarifications and outcomes.
